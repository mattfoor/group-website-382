In his free time, Carmy is working on a quirky new phone case design for Acme, or cooking some fancy dish inspired by his time studying abroad.

Co-Founder and Head of Operations

Sydney followed Carmy out of Harvard and into Acme. Sydney is really the glue that keeps the team together and is a hard but fair task master.
